CONTRIBUTE

Say your piece to the KLASS community.


STEP 1

Fill in the form below indicating your name, tutor group, proposed article title and a brief one or two sentence summary regarding your topic of choice in the form below.

STEP 2

Await approval from us. We're happy to pick ideas that are intriguing and unique, whilst also being appropriate for the school. In other words, if you wouldn't read your article to Mr Slade, then it probably shouldn't be on here.

STEP 3

Once you have the green light to proceed with the article, send the final draft and a short bio about you to any of the emails below. We're very flexible with deadlines, but we would like to see it at least one to two weeks after approval. We'll edit and send it for approval to the school.

STEP 4

If Alice Smith approves of the article, we'll contact you and put it on our short list. We update the front page every 2 weeks or whenever we have 7 articles in our short list, so sit tight for now.

STEP 5

Congratulations on becoming a KLASS blogger! Check out your article on the front page and your own personalised bio page. Contact us below if you'd like to become a regular blogger for the site.
